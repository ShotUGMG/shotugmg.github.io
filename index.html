Introduction

Survival Reimagined is a realism difficulty scaling mod by changing progression to achieve realism. Progress the ages starting at stone to bronze to steel all the way to diamond. Efficiently maintain your hunger and stay out of the darkness. Discover deeper depths beneath the deepslate.
